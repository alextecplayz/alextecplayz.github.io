I'm the most comfortable with GDScript, C++, HTML, CSS and JS, and Liquid. I don't use web frameworks like React, Angular, Vue, and so on.

I like to tinker with stuff a lot, so you can expect posts on Masto or on my blog about experiments and whatnot.

I stopped talking about work - not because there's anything wrong with it or whatever, I just have a tendency to ramble about things and office work doesn't really seem all that noteworthy for this blog.
