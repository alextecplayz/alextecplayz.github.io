- [SUCKERPUNCH](https://music.youtube.com/playlist?list=OLAK5uy_k2f_V49cinYk4TqJlbODubGZOcjR6Fi1g) - 39 minutes - **(9/10)** - Maggie Lindemann's SUCKERPUNCH is a full-course meal with highlights such as "take me nowhere", "she knows it", "i'm so lonely with you" and "self sabotage", followed by "break me!", "girl next door", "hear me out" and "how could you do this to me?". girl next door is my fourth most played song in my Top 250 on Metrolist, for reference.

This is what you get when you combine Metro Exodus, Entropy Zero 2, electronic rock and guitar, some melancholic and hopelessness mixed in. So, so recommend listening to it!
