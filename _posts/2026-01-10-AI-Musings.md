**The solution to AI music on YouTube Music?** I [wrote a userscript, YTM Hide](https://techhub.social/@alextecplayz/115385784574924119) which is available [now](https://github.com/alextecplayz/userscripts/blob/main/userscript_ytmusic-block-items.js) for both Desktop by installing it via your userscript manager of choice (e.g. ViolentMonkey) and mobile through Cromite, with its built-in support for userscripts.

In YouTube Music's settings, there's a new YTM Hide tab where you can manage what is blocked in the list, you can reset it, you can copy the content inside the list and share it with others, do whatever.

Right-click albums to hide them specifically, click or tap on the three-dot button to open an options menu to blacklist the channel as a whole. Hides AI slop, shit music and anything else you don't want to see, by outright removing it from the website's HTML. It won't remove them from the song queue, maybe I'll get to doing that someday, but it's significantly better to see that out of my entire Explore page, the only releases worth my fucking time are like 5 or so, while the rest is slop and AI.

It's not a perfect solution, but it's better than nothing! And we need more such userscripts, for other platforms!
