What is Frosted Planet?

Frosted Planet was a very unfinished idea about arctic exploration on a newly-discovered planet that could be potentially terraformed and be inhabited by humans, as Earth is beginning to collapse due to global warming and catastrophes caused by climate change. In the beginning, the player arrives on a frozen continent and sets up a base for research, exploration and expansion.

Over time, the player would be able to travel further, and reach places that weren't frozen, such as an overgrown jungle with an abandoned research lab that would become a secondary base, a desert where the player could set up a third base in a hill, and a tall mountain, where the player could set up their final base, that would offer spectacular views both from the side, and the peak of the mountain.

Other information

Ultimately, Frosted Planet was scrapped, for the time being.

 

I really don't think Frosted Planet is a bad idea for a game, but it would take a long time to develop, especially as it's supposed to be somwhat open-world and expansive, with multiple locations and mechanics.

Frosted Planet was inspired by ASTRONEER, Scrap Mechanic and Raft.
